# v1.2.0

## Features
- **In-app changelog.** Click the version in the status bar to open a "What's New" panel with every release's notes — bundled with the app, no network needed.
- **Pan and zoom photos.** The fullscreen image viewer now zooms toward your cursor (scroll or trackpad-pinch), pans by dragging, and toggles fit/zoom on double-click.
- **Clear buttons on inputs.** Every text field — search boxes, filters, forms — shows an X to clear it in one click.
- **New logo and name.** Shows has an equalizer-bars logo (it tints to your accent in the sidebar), and the app is now properly capitalized as "Shows".

## Improvements
- The Artists, Venues, Locations, and Friends lists keep your search and sort when you open an entry and come back — matching the events list.
- Autocomplete fields behave better: the menu stays open when your text exactly matches an option, arrow keys scroll the highlighted row into view, and tabbing to the next field closes the menu instead of leaving every dropdown open.
- Right-clicking no longer pops the browser's default menu, clearing the way for custom menus later.

## Fixes
- Counts now reflect only shows you've actually attended — past and non-cancelled. Dashboard totals, the Artists/Venues/Locations/Friends counts, and the artist-page stats no longer inflate with upcoming or cancelled events.
- Editing an event's artists, venue, or location updates the existing records instead of spawning duplicates as you type.
- Fixed outdated guidance that pointed at a "Fetch all" button for genre tags.

## Notes
- After updating, the app appears as **Shows** (capitalized) with the new icon. The rename changes the installer/app-bundle names; your data is unaffected.
